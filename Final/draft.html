Welcome to Blu Ray Reboot!

If you are a film lover and are still watching DVDs, it's time to reboot your collection and upgrade to Blu Ray! What's better about Blu Ray? Mainly image quality, sound quality, and disc special features. Our site provides all the information about Blu Ray someone looking to switch over from DVD would need to know about Blu Ray Discs and players!

Be sure to check out the image quality comparison page which features a rollover image gallery and still frame zoom functionality for up close image comparison! Final site will include such helpful features as:

  • Spec Comparison Guide
  • Blu Ray vs DVD Audio Quality Comparisons
  • Zoom Image Quality Comparisons
  • Full List of Featured Titles With Links To Individual Information Pages

This site aims to show film enthusiasts just what they're missing if they aren't watching Blu Rays.
