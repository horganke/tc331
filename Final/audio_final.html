Comparing audio quality

Blu Ray's high capacity disc allows for lossless audio and a higher quality sound. Check out some examples of Dolby Digital sound found on DVDs compared to Dolby TrueHD found on Blu Ray. You can listen to some examples of this below. Use the controls to play, pause, and raise and lower the volume of the clips

This is an audio clip from the Life of Pi DVD. Pay attention to the tigers roar and the sound of flying fish.

Now listen to the scene from the Blu Ray. Notice how the tigers roar is much less muted and much more threatening, and the flying fish whizzing by sound crisp, clear, and all around you.

Here's another example, an audio clip from the Chronicle DVD. Listen to this scene involving a car crash.

Now listen to the Blu Ray version. The sounds of shattering glass and the dialogue are much clearer and more distinct. Much like Blu Rays picture, the audio had much more crispness and clarity to it than the DVD version
